CUSTOMER CHURN PREDICTION SYSTEM

Hi !!! Here is a simple model to determine if the customer will churn or not.

FEATURES

INTRODUCTION Churn prediction is really essential for banks to determine whether the customer will leave the bank or not. Since customers are the most valuable assets to any company , it becomes imperative to build a strong model to predict their retention.The analysis obtained is a key business metric , because the cost of retaining existing customers is far less than acquiring a new one. In addition , long term customers provide more profit.

APPROACH

The model has been built on a existing dataset , where several features of the customer help to determine his/her churn status.The target variable is categorical in nature. [Y or N ]

METHOD

This model has been built after testing different approaches like logistic regression , decision tree. The final model used is based on Random Forest.

ACCURACY

This model provides good prediction with low error.

UTILITY

Easy to use model. Simply put in the numerical details of the customer in their respective domains and the model will predict churn status .

Enter the details.

Only enter numerical values. For domains regarding Yes/No , type 1 for Yes and 0 for No.
